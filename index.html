A sanctuary in progress

A resort born from forest, light and calm.

Green Heaven is being thoughtfully completed as a retreat that places nature at the centre of every stay. The first A-frame residence offers a preview of an atmosphere shaped by timber warmth, secluded evenings and an immersive green setting.

Green Heaven Resort

Intimate by design, immersed in nature.

Envisioned as a peaceful resort escape, Green Heaven will bring guests closer to Sri Lanka's natural quiet through characterful cabin stays and warm, personal hospitality. The illuminated A-frame now taking shape offers a first indication of the resort's considered architectural style.

This website introduces the developing concept. Room types, inclusions, dining, activities, rates and opening schedules remain subject to completion and confirmation by the resort.
