Explore the Region

Places to Visit Nearby

Green Heaven is placed close to Habarana nature, Sigiriya heritage and some of the best wildlife experiences in the cultural triangle.

Pidurangala Rock - 9.3 km

A famous rock viewpoint with sunrise scenery and wide views across the Sigiriya landscape.

Sigiriya Temple - 9.67 km

A cultural stop close to the resort area, ideal for guests exploring Sigiriya and its surroundings.

Minneriya National Park - 12.12 km

A popular wildlife destination known for jeep safaris, elephants and dry-zone nature experiences.

Habarana Lake - 1.19 km

A peaceful local lake area for soft nature views, relaxed walks and quiet moments close to the resort.

Kaudulla National Park - 22 km

A dry-zone wildlife reserve often paired with Minneriya for elephant watching and guided safari drives.

Dambulla Cave Temple - 23.31 km

A UNESCO-recognised cave temple complex with ancient Buddhist art, statues and a strong cultural atmosphere.
